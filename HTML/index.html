Superscript / Subscript Tag

Superscript and subscript are used to format text in a specific way.

Superscript appears above the normal line of text, while subscript appears below it.

Superscript is often used for exponents, while subscript is used for chemical formulas or mathematical notation.

For example, H2O is the chemical formula for water, and E=mc2 is Einstein's famous equation.

iFrame Tag

The iFrame tag is used to embed another HTML document within the current document.

It allows you to display content from another source, such as a webpage or a video, within your own page.
